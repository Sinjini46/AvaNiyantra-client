About

Learn More About Us

AvaNiyantra, loosely by Disarticulation of Words here means Narcotic Control as Avamohan means Narcotics in Sanskrit and Niyantran means control in the same, is the application developed to tackle the problem of tracking huge consignments of Narcotic drugs. The main ambition of this application is to keep the drugs from getting lost or stolen in the reason of any illegal activity, while in transit from the manufacturer to the local pharmacists. This aims to reduce the loss of Narcotic and Psychotropic Drugs ultimately dealing a huge blow to those performing illegal misuse of the said drugs
